Our Story

The cradle where Albursa has been founded and developed was a unique experience in practicing the construction business at all levels, in addition to the special experience obtained from travelling all over the world discovering the new technologies and other cultures related to the same fields. Albursa was founded from construction backgrounds in order to work for real-estate and construction fields. We aim to improve and develop the practice of construction businesses in all regions to be in line with the latest technologies and to match with the existing economic conditions.

Our Vision

The world is a small village connected in a smart network. All information available for all. We just make it easy to be found.

Our Mission

If you want to build to last, you must be honest and you should keep trying to step towards perfection. The success is to deliver a proper service for whom in need for this service. It is not a kind of intelligence to convince anyone to pay for a service they do not need. Albursa puts its data base available for whom in need whatever because they don’t have enough time to go through the browsers or they don’t know the proper product or client for their project. It is a supreme goal that you have a footprint in spreading culture and science in one of the fields of specialization as widely as possible.

Our Goal

We aim to improve and develop the practice of construction businesses in all regions to be in line with the latest technologies and to match with the existing economic conditions. Albursa aims to create a special partnership with all parties involved in the construction and real-estate businesses such as developers, designers, manufacturers, consultants, suppliers, services providers, contractors, owners and end users. A huge network of designers, consultants, real estate developers, contractors, services providers, manufacturers, suppliers…etc. shall be available for cooperation together and providing their services and products to end users whatever they are developers, owners, contractors, traders…. etc. We make all resources from all over the world available for everyone involved in the construction and real estate fields all over the globe, so they can easily achieve the best of quality, cost and time. All contact details of all network members shall be available for free for all registered users because we believe the direct contact is the best way of communications and achieving targets. The possibility to make an international Tender for your project became very easy through Albursa, which makes the delivery easier at the optimum standards and requirments. Albursa has a FORUM available for all users for gathering and sharing construction business knowledge and brain storming in aim of improving and strengthen the scientific part of real estate and construction business.
